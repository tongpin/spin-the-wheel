About Spin the Wheel

Spin the Wheel is a free decision wheel and random picker built for speed, trust, and visual quality. It is designed for educators, streamers, teams, communities, and anyone who needs a clear and fair random choice.

Who it is for

This wheel works best anywhere a quick, neutral choice keeps things moving. It fits classrooms, meetings, livestreams, community events, shops, and everyday home decisions.

  • Teachers running classroom selection and activities.
  • Event organizers and giveaway hosts who need transparent fairness.
  • Teams choosing tasks, topics, or rotations.
  • Creators and streamers who want a polished on-screen wheel.

What is the wheel spinner for?

  • Picking the next student, volunteer, or speaker without awkward guesswork.
  • Running simple draws for customers, attendees, or people who completed a survey.
  • Deciding who starts in a standup, game night, or group activity.
  • Breaking small deadlocks, like which task to tackle first or what to eat tonight.

How to use

  1. Add entries manually, paste in bulk, or load a built-in template.
  2. Customize mode, speed, theme, and sound.
  3. Spin by clicking the wheel, pressing the center button, or using Ctrl/Cmd + K.
  4. Review results history and share the wheel URL when needed.

Product principles

  • Fairness: cryptographic randomness with visible transparency messaging.
  • Privacy: wheel data stays local by default; Cloudflare Web Analytics measures aggregate site usage.
  • No friction: no account required, no ads, and immediate first spin.
